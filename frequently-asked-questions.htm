General Program Information

What is the LivePerson Affiliate Program?

LivePerson’s LiveEngage is the best platform for customer service agents to communicate with customers. For over 20 years, we’ve worked with thousands of successful companies, making us one of the largest and most respected live chat and messaging solutions on the market. Our user-friendly solution appeals to a wide variety of industries and organizations, ranging from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies. If you are a blogger, consultant, freelancer, developer, agency or industry influencer you can get rewarded from LivePerson’s Affiliate Program.

The LivePerson Affiliate Program allows you or your organization to earn commissions for referring customers to LivePerson. As an affiliate, you’ll have access to marketing material such as banners, prewritten text, and tracked links you can place on your site and/or newsletter. Through these links, customers will be directed to our site and tagged with your affiliate account. Upon conversion to a paid account, you’ll receive an $100 referral fee. It’s really that easy!

What is the difference between LiveEngage and LivePerson?

When we say LivePerson, it refers to our company. LiveEngage is our software which supports activities like messaging and customer engagement.

How do I join the LivePerson Affiliate Program?

Create an account through our Get Ambassador portal. Once you register, you can start promoting LiveEngage right away. We will provide marketing material for your use, but you can choose to create your own. While you wait for customers to register for LiveEngage using your tracked links, complete your profile with the email linked to your PayPal account. PayPal is currently the only method we use to distribute funds.

How does the LivePerson Affiliate Program work?

The LivePerson Affiliate program rewards you commissions for every customer you refer to LivePerson through your affiliate link and convert into a paying customer. Here’s how to get started:
1. Create a LivePerson Affiliate Program Account
2. Promote LiveEngage using your unique Affiliate Link
3. Use the Get Ambassador portal to track links placed on your website, newsletter, social media, etc.
4. Get rewarded for each paying customer you refer.
5. Log in anytime to check the status of your commissions earned.

Does it cost me anything to become a LivePerson Affiliate?

No, it’s free to sign up and become an affiliate partner!

What are my responsibilities as a LivePerson Affiliate?

As an affiliate, it’s your responsibility to promote LiveEngage to your audience including your existing user base and future prospects. How you want to promote LiveEngage is up to you! If you’re not sure about what’s accepted or not, please refer to Terms and Conditions.

Whom should I contact if I have questions about the LivePerson Affiliate Program?

Please email the LivePerson’s Affiliate Team at affiliates@liveperson.com.

Setting up Links

After I join the LivePerson’s Affiliate Program, how do I get the ads and links to put on my site?

Access your Affiliate Link
1. Log in to LivePerson’s GetAmbassador portal.
2. From your Dashboard, you’ll find the Campaign URL field in the upper right corner.
3. The URL is your affiliate link, be sure to use this link as is on your website, otherwise, you may not be rewarded for your referrals. The more qualified traffic you bring through this link, the more opportunities you’ll get at earning referral fees, so be sure you place it in high-traffic areas of your website.

Access Affiliate Marketing Assets
1. Log in to LivePerson’s GetAmbassador portal.
2. Open the “Assets” tab.
3. Download any marketing material asset you need. Additionally, you can download our “Get Started Guide” for more marketing ideas.

Sales & Commissions

How much can I earn?

There is no cap to the commissions you can earn. The more customers you refer to LivePerson, the opportunities you’ll have to generate revenue.

How do I know how much commission I’ve earned?

Within the affiliate partner portal, you can access the following tabs and information:
- Dashboard (Get a quick glimpse of your profile.)
- Stats (See how much you’ve earned from our affiliate program and filter by payout amounts or earned amounts.)
- Commissions (View each customer you’ve referred to LivePerson.)
- Payouts (Review payments and verify your earnings.)
- Assets (Download pre-designed ads to start promoting.)

How am I paid?

You will receive a deposit into your paypal account for every paying customers 30 days from the day they sign up. You will not receive referral fees for free trial sign-ups.

Who processes orders and handles customer service?

We will take care of processing orders, managing, and supporting the customer. All you do is place the link on your site and promote LiveEngage; we’ll take care of the rest.

How will LivePerson know that the creation of a new account came from my site?

By using your affiliate link on your site, the GetAmbassador system can track customers that arrive at LivePerson from your site. It’s why it’s very important that you use the right affiliate link.

I’ve stopped receiving emails about my commission. How do I get them back?

To ensure you receive all the emails regarding the LivePerson Affiliate Program, please add affiliates@liveperson.com to your contacts or Safe List. To view and access previous emails, please check your Spam box.
