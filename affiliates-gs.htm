5 tips for becoming a successful affiliate

  1. Promote LiveEngage in high-traffic areas. The more visibility your referral link gets, the higher the chance someone will create a new LiveEngage account with your unique affiliate link. This mean, more opportunities for you to earn commissions.
  2. Leverage the LivePerson Brand. We’ve created blog posts, emails, social media posts and graphics for you to use. You’ll also get access to our logo. Use these assets and the brand to drive traffic to the LiveEngage technology.
  3. Provide Value to Customers. You know your audience best. Provide value to your customers by identify their pain points then showing them how LiveEngage can help overcome their problems. Your customers will thank you!
  4. Understand the Product. The best way to sell something, is to not sell it at all. The best marketing comes from genuine people who believe in the products they are promoting. If you don’t know our platform already, visit our website, chat with us, or sign up for a free trial and take our platform for a spin.
  5. Follow the rules. If you have any questions about whether something does or doesn’t fall within the rules, ask. Getting penalized by search engines or having your account deactivated can result in loss conversion. Our rules are outlined in our Terms and Conditions. Have additional questions? Referral fees

Once you sign up for our affiliate program, you can start earning referral fees immediately.

  1. Complete your profile by adding your PayPal account email.
  2. Once a referred customer signs up for their LiveEngage free trial, your referral fee will be placed in a “pending” status. When they become a paying customer after the trial period, the referral will become “approved.” If they do not become a paying customer, the referral fee will be “denied.”
  3. Referral fees for approved accounts will be paid 30 days after the account is paid.
